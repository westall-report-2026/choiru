2. Vision & Mission

Vision

To strengthen Westall as a welcoming, sustainable, and active community centre that supports religious growth, education, youth development, and community engagement.

Mission

  • To provide consistent religious, educational, and social programs for the community.
  • To improve organisational structure, reporting, and internal coordination.
  • To encourage youth involvement and volunteer participation.
  • To strengthen financial sustainability and transparent planning.
  • To improve utilisation of facilities for beneficial community programs.
